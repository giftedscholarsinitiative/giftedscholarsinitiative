The Program

STEM Gifted Program for Grades 6-8 🎓

Program Overview:

Our STEM Gifted Program is designed to challenge and nurture high-ability students, helping them develop critical thinking, problem-solving, and innovation skills. Through a carefully structured curriculum that includes AI, cognitive mapping, and entrepreneurial thinking, students will explore advanced concepts beyond the traditional school curriculum. The program encourages creativity, leadership, and resilience, ensuring that gifted students are not just learning but excelling at their true potential.

Eligibility: Students in grades 6-8 with strong analytical thinking, problem-solving skills, and a keen interest in STEM.

Duration: 4 weeks - 3 x 1.5 hour sessions each week - Sunday, Wednesday, Friday

Rewards: Gifted Scholars Initiative program completion certificate + early access to the next GSI program

Week 1: The Science of Thinking 🧠

Focus: Critical Thinking, Analytical Reasoning, and Problem-Solving

Students will master logical reasoning and inquiry-based learning through interactive activities:

  • 🔑 Escape Room Challenge – Solve logic puzzles to "break out" of a virtual room.
  • 🎤 Socratic Debate – Engage in deep scientific discussions using the Parlay Ideas tool.
  • 🧩 Deductive Reasoning Puzzles – Live problem-solving sessions with lateral thinking challenges.

Week 2: AI & Robotics 🤖

Focus: Artificial Intelligence, Coding, Computational Thinking

Students explore AI, machine learning, and robotics with hands-on projects:

  • 🤖 Build an AI Chatbot – Create a chatbot using Scratch AI extensions.
  • ⚖ AI Ethics Debate – Discuss AI’s impact on society and its ethical implications.
  • 📸 AI Image Recognition – Train an AI model to recognize objects through Google Teachable Machine.

Week 3: Engineering for a Better World 🔧

Focus: Sustainable Engineering, Design Thinking, Prototyping

Students will apply engineering principles to create innovative solutions:

  • 🏗 Design a 3D Prototype – Create a futuristic sustainable house using Tinkercad.
  • 🔬 Reverse Engineering – Dissect virtual models of bridges, circuits, and turbines.
  • 🏙 Eco-Friendly City Planning – Design a smart city with renewable energy sources.

Week 4: The Entrepreneurial Mind 💡

Focus: Leadership, Public Speaking, and Innovation

Students develop entrepreneurial skills and prepare for the future:

  • 🎤 Shark Tank Pitch Challenge – Present innovative STEM business ideas.
  • 🎙 The Persuasive Power of Storytelling – Write and illustrate digital STEM stories.
  • 📑 AI-Powered Resume & LinkedIn Simulation – Create futuristic resumes as STEM pioneers.
