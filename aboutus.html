Meet the Team

The passionate minds behind the Gifted Education Summer Program

Kaartik Issar

Founder & Mentor (University of Toronto Alumni)

A former gifted student himself, Kaartik brings a deep, personal understanding of what it takes to nurture talent. A high distinction graduate from the University of Toronto in Computer Science, he has been tutoring gifted students for over two years. Now working in the AI and tech industry in Toronto, he bridges academic excellence with real-world experience. Kaartik’s vision is to create a program that challenges young minds and helps them flourish with support, creativity, and structure—just as he once needed and received.

Kriti Issar

Curriculum Architect & Innovation Lead (Harvard'26 x PhD, University of Delhi)

With over five years of experience in transforming classrooms through research-based strategies, Kriti brings unmatched insight into educational innovation. Currently a Ph.D. scholar in Early Childhood Education at the University of Delhi, she’s deeply invested in building equitable and inspiring learning spaces. She has received offers from Harvard, Columbia, and UPenn, and will soon join Harvard for a master’s in Education Policy and Analysis. Kriti’s expertise ensures our curriculum is not just engaging and advanced, but also inclusive and developmentally aligned for gifted learners.
