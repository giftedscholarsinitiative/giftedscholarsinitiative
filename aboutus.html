Meet the Team

The passionate minds behind the Gifted Education Summer Program

Kaartik Issar

Founder & Mentor (Data Scientist & University of Toronto Alumni)

A former gifted student himself, Kaartik brings a deep, personal understanding of what it takes to nurture talent. A high distinction graduate from the University of Toronto in Computer Science (#12th best CS program in the world), he has been tutoring gifted students for over three years. A former Teaching Assistant at University of Toronto, Kaartik is now working in the AI and tech industry in Toronto, he bridges academic excellence with real-world experience. Kaartik’s vision is to create a program that challenges young minds and helps them flourish with support, creativity, and structure—just as he once needed and received.

Kriti Issar

Curriculum Architect & Innovation Lead (PhD, University of Delhi x Harvard'26)

With over five years of experience in transforming classrooms through research-based strategies, Kriti brings unmatched insight into educational innovation. Currently a Ph.D. scholar in Early Childhood Education at the University of Delhi, she’s deeply invested in building equitable and inspiring learning spaces. She has received offers from Harvard, Columbia, and UPenn, and will soon join Harvard for a master’s in Education Policy and Analysis. Kriti’s expertise ensures our curriculum is not just engaging and advanced, but also inclusive and developmentally aligned for gifted learners.

Zackary Masri

Education Strategist (Analyst & Master's student @Georgia Tech)

Zackary Masri brings a wealth of academic depth and real-world analytical experience to the team. A graduate of McMaster University with dual Bachelor's degrees in Statistics and Business Administration, he is currently pursuing 2 Master’s degrees—one in Computer Science at Georgia Tech and another in Financial Engineering from WorldQuant University. With over three years of tutoring experience and a current role as a tech analyst, Zackary is passionate about the intersection of education, data, and innovation. His commitment to lifelong learning and cross-disciplinary expertise makes him a driving force in crafting transformative educational journeys for students.

Dr. Meenakshi Gupta

Academic Advisor (Principal, MIEE, MIE Trainer, Executive member of NPSC and GIEN)

Dr. Gupta brings 28 years of rich experience in education across teaching, school leadership, and teacher training. Currently serving as the Principal of a reputed public school in Delhi, she has previously held positions such as Vice Principal and Academic Coordinator. As a Microsoft Innovative Educator Expert (MIEE) and Trainer, as well as an executive member of NPSC and GIEN , she actively contributes to future-forward teaching practices. A published author of multiple English textbooks, Dr. Gupta advises the team on educational strategy, curriculum relevance, and scalable school engagement models.

Dr. Madhukar Sharma

Research Advisor (Associate Professor @NLU)

With over two decades of combined government and academic experience, Dr. Madhukar Sharma is a distinguished figure in legal education. Currently an Associate Professor at a prestigious National Law University, Dr. Sharma has led numerous empirical research projects on pressing societal issues. His innovative training programs have pioneered experiential learning in universities. As an advisor, Dr. Sharma provides invaluable insights into research methodology, curriculum design, and pedagogical innovation, helping shape a future-ready educational model.
